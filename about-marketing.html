About Piyumika Dissanayake

Piyumika Dissanayake is our Senior UI/UX Engineer & Digital Strategist — a versatile web technology specialist whose international career has spanned multi-national organisations across Sweden, Norway, Saudi Arabia, and Sri Lanka. She brings a rare fusion of frontend engineering precision and user experience design thinking to every digital project she leads.

With deep hands-on experience building complex web applications — including online booking systems, Learning Management Systems (LMS), and Electronic Health Record (EHR) platforms — Piyumika understands what it takes to create digital products that are not only beautiful but engineered for performance, accessibility, and conversion. Her mobile development background using Apache Cordova further extends her capability across web and hybrid application platforms.

"A great digital product is not just about what it looks like — it's about how effortlessly it connects a user's intent to the result they need. Every interface decision is a business decision, and every pixel should earn its place."

— Piyumika Dissanayake, Senior UI/UX Engineer & Digital Strategist

Piyumika is also a published author on digital topics, sharing insights on SEO fundamentals and web accessibility through UX Tribe and Medium — reflecting her commitment to the intersection of technical excellence and digital discoverability. Her accessibility-first design philosophy ensures that the digital experiences she builds reach and serve the broadest possible audience.

Piyumika approaches every digital engagement as a product engineering challenge — systematically aligning user needs, business objectives, and technical constraints to produce digital experiences that are beautiful, fast, accessible, and conversion-optimised.

Discovery — User & Market Research: Before any design or code, we understand your users deeply — their mental models, pain points, and goals. Combined with competitive analysis and SEO landscape mapping, this shapes every subsequent decision.
