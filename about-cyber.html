Section 04

Security-First Methodology

Dr. Sharma's security engagements follow a military-precision framework she calls RAPID — Reconnaissance, Assessment, Protection, Intelligence, and Defence-in-Depth.

Reconnaissance: We begin by mapping your entire attack surface — every endpoint, user, application, and third-party connection — before your adversary does. Our AI-powered scanning identifies vulnerabilities that traditional tools miss.

Assessment: A full enterprise risk assessment quantifies your exposure in financial terms your board will understand, prioritizing remediations by potential impact rather than technical severity alone.

Protection: We design and implement layered security controls — from Zero Trust network architecture to endpoint detection, privileged access management, and encryption-at-rest.

Intelligence: Our real-time threat intelligence feeds, powered by AI, continuously monitor the dark web, threat actor groups, and emerging exploit databases relevant to your industry.

Defence-in-Depth: Regular red-team exercises, tabletop simulations, and staff security training ensure your human layer is as resilient as your technical one.
