About Iroshan Aberathne

Iroshan Aberathne is our AI & Software Engineering Architect — a PhD-qualified researcher, data scientist, and engineering leader whose career spans academic lecturing, fintech data science leadership, and senior software architecture roles at high-growth technology companies across the Asia-Pacific region.

Currently serving as Architect in Software Engineering & Data Science at Sinorbis — a leading digital experience platform focused on the Chinese market — Iroshan brings a rare combination of deep theoretical grounding in artificial intelligence and hands-on production engineering experience. His career has taken him from Quality Assurance at Virtusa through technical leadership roles, a Senior Lecturer position in Information Technology, and data science leadership as Assistant Vice President at Softlogic Life Insurance.

"The most powerful AI systems are not the ones built with the most complex algorithms — they're the ones engineered with clarity of purpose, rigorous data practices, and a deep understanding of the human problems they're solving."

Iroshan Aberathne, AI & Software Engineering Architect

Iroshan completed his PhD in Artificial Intelligence at Lincoln University, New Zealand, where his research focused on applying ML to real-world engineering and mathematical problems. Prior to that, he held an M.Phil in Engineering Mathematics from the University of Peradeniya — a foundation that informs his uniquely rigorous, mathematically-grounded approach to AI system design.
